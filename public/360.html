PROJECT WOLBACHIA - SINGAPORE'S

FROM EGG

TO ADULT

Click into the rooms to learn about the steps in the rearing of male Wolbachia-Aedes mosquitoes. Many of the innovations you will see were developed in-house with our collaborator, Orinno Technology Pte. Ltd, to make it possible for us to scale up production.

Adult Room

Adult male and female Wolbachia-Aedes mosquitoes are reared to produce large numbers of eggs.

Larvae Room

Mosquito eggs are hatched and Wolbachia-Aedes larvae are reared to the pupae stage under controlled environmental conditions.

Sorting Room

Male pupae are smaller than female pupae and can be sorted by size. Sorting is done with very high accuracy, leaving only very few females.

Emergence Room

Irradiated pupae are packed into release devices and allowed to emerge into adult Wolbachia-Aedes mosquitoes for release in the field.

Quality Control Room

Regular laboratory testing is conducted to ensure that the male Wolbachia-Aedes mosquitoes we release continue to carry Wolbachia.

Field Release

Male Wolbachia-Aedes aegypti mosquitoes are released into the community to suppress the urban dengue mosquito populations.

X-Ray Room

Sorted pupae are exposed to a low dose of X-ray radiation. This sterialises any remaining few female Wolbachia-Aedes.
