Welcome to Project Wolbachia - Singapore's

Mosquito Production Facility

The National Environment Agency's Project Wolbachia — Singapore involves the release of male Wolbachia-Aedes mosquitoes to suppress urban dengue mosquito populations. Join us for a behind-the-scenes look at our unique facility, and learn how male Wolbachia-Aedes mosquitoes are produced and released.
